# Legend of the Red Bull — Roadmap (v1.5 & v2)

## Overview
This document outlines the post-v1 roadmap for Legend of the Red Bull.  
v1.5 focuses on quality-of-life, small expansions, and optional features.  
v2 introduces major systems such as tournaments, PvP, advanced gear, and boss pathways.

---

## v1.5 — Small Expansions & QoL

### 1. Expanded Gear Shop
- More sneaker types with unique modifiers  
- Accessories with minor buffs  
- Limited-use “skill books” (shooting clinic, dunk camp)

### 2. Improved Club 23
- More rumor text  
- Bartender quest chain  
- Better gambling UI  

### 3. Additional Street Events
- Short narrative encounters  
- Rival ballers  
- Sponsor callbacks  
- Red Bull foreshadowing events

### 4. Optional Stamina System
- Light fatigue mechanic  
- Gym and Bar interactions have more meaning  

### 5. Player History / Stats Screen Improvements
- More achievements  
- Win streaks  
- Highest-scoring games recorded  

---

## v2 — Major Features

### 1. Tournaments / Ladder System
- Multi-game sequences  
- Prize pools  
- Rep-heavy rewards  
- Unlocks special courts  

### 2. Ghost PvP (“Async PvP”)
- Players challenge stored builds  
- No concurrency or live multiplayer needed  
- Rankings and daily MVPs  

### 3. Advanced Gear Customization
- Sneaker Customizer (risk-based upgrades)  
- Mod slots for accessories  
- Rare items found through quests  

### 4. Expanded Narrative Pathways
- Long story chains  
- Archetype-specific quests  
- Street-legends and mini-bosses  

### 5. Encounter Overhaul
- Larger data-driven event tables  
- Weighted RNG  
- Combo events / multi-choice outcomes  

### 6. Red Bull Final Path v2
- Multi-stage boss sequence  
- Dream-game sequences  
- Alternate endings

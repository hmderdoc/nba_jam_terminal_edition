# Legend of the Red Bull — Rim City Lore & Flavor Bible

## Overview
This document establishes the narrative tone, recurring motifs, and flavor used across the game.

It is not a strict requirement but helps keep all writing consistent across modules generated by AI tools.

---

## 1. Tone

- **Mythic basketball surrealism**  
- **Urban fantasy** with arcade DNA  
- 90s NBA Jam energy meets LoRD parody  
- Lighthearted, not grim  
- Occasional inspirational Jordan-mythos moments  

---

## 2. Rim City

A sprawling, legendary city where:

- Streetball is a religion  
- Courts feel alive  
- Every alley hides a hustler or a prophet  
- Legends walk like urban demigods  

Key districts:

- **Courtline Avenue** — the heart of open run culture  
- **Dunk District** — neon-lit asphalt, home of high-fliers  
- **The Arc** — sniper’s haven, respected shooters roam  
- **Club 23** — rumors swirl like smoke  
- **Rim City Gear Shop** — treasure trove of questionable footwear  
- **The Gym** — sweat = currency  

---

## 3. The Red Bull

A mythical figure:
- Equal parts Michael Jordan, urban legend, and supernatural force  
- Sometimes described as “six rings forged into a spirit”  
- Sometimes as a man who drank a forbidden pre-ban Red Bull and ascended  

Players do not simply *fight* the Red Bull.  
They attempt to **earn the right** to face him.

Encounters are rare, hinted through:
- Omen events  
- Bartender rumors  
- Dream sequences  
- Legendary streetball sightings  

---

## 4. Street Legends (Future Expansion Hooks)

Characters who can appear in events or tournaments:

- **Fadeaway Prophet** — old man who teaches timing  
- **The Rimkeeper** — enigmatic custodian of Court 9  
- **Sole Collector** — sneaker hoarder with cursed inventory  
- **Neon Gator** — dancer/baller, brings chaos and luck  

---

## 5. Items (Flavor Guide)

Sneakers:
- “Rim Grinders” — heavy, ugly, unstoppable  
- “Cloudwalkers” — rumored to give mid-air control  
- “Air Relic” — said to carry echoes of the Red Bull’s footsteps  

Drinks:
- “Red Bull Classic (Pre-Ban)”  
- “Mystery Mix” — concoction of questionable legality  

Accessories:
- “Mamba Wraps”  
- “Wrist Rockets”  
- “Focus Bands”  

---

## 6. Writing Style Guidelines for AI Tools

- Maintain light humor  
- Use basketball metaphors as fantasy elements  
- Keep text concise and punchy  
- Lean into absurdity when appropriate  
- Always respect the mythos of “The Red Bull”
